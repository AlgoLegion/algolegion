Who Are We

AlgoLegion is an E-learning platform that helps you determine your career path and allows you to learn from real-life Software Developers & Data Scientists.

Studies suggest that about 80% of the engineers find it hard to learn Industry-Relavant skills before they graduate. If this prevails, tech-companies have no choice but to limit their rate of recruitment.

Our Expertise

AlgoLegion can help you go from an absolute Beginner to a confident Intermediate in less than 48 hours of learning.

We owe it to our student-friendly tutors and industry-relavant accelerated coursework. We always start from the basics so you don't have to worry about lagging behind. But instead of wasting a whole lot of time in the basics, we help you build real-world applications with the things that you've learnt.

One-day Virtual Workshops

Organised mostly on weekends, our one-day workshops are something which students can make the most out of. We focus on subjects such as Python, R, Java, SQL, Web Development etc., with a clear syllabus that's split efficiently throughout the day.

Programming Bootcamps

If you're looking forward to get a deeper understanding in vast subjects such as C, C++, Java, Javascript, OOPS & Python but prefer a slow pace, our bootcamps is what you should go for. Here, you'll have small educational sessions that last for 2 - 3 days.

Career Mentorships

Every student has to find their way into the job market sooner or later. AlgoLegion can help you land in a job where you won't just fit in but also love what you do. Our academicians and industry experts can guide you personally through 1-on-1 meetings.

Internships

Industry would only respect hands-on work experience rather than educational qualifications. Work with us for a week and learn what it takes to be an intern. Remember, you'll never know how good you are, unless you actually start doing what you've learned.

Student Community

Be a part of AlgoLegion's growing student community. Interact with students who share similar interests with you in both education and career. Apart from knowledge exchange, this is a great way to grow and extent your network & connections.

Competitions

AlgoLegion organises frequent paper presentations and project displays which gives you a great opportunity to share your knowledge in tech. Every top performer and participant is awarded with a certificate to showcase your victory to the world.
